About


Welcome to our food donation site! Our mission is to fight hunger by connecting individuals, restaurants, and other food establishments with local charities and organizations that provide food assistance to those in need.

By bringing together donors and charities in one easy-to-use platform, we are making a positive impact on the lives of those in need and helping to build stronger, more resilient communities.

Thank you for joining us in the fight against hunger & food wastage. Together, we can make a difference!
